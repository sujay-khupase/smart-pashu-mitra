What this platform does

Six modules, one register

📋

Farmer Registration

Keep a simple digital record of every farmer's name, village, and contact details.

🐄

Animal Registration

Link each animal to its owner with breed, age, and weight on record.

💉

Vaccination Tracker

See upcoming and overdue vaccinations at a glance, sorted by due date.

🩺

Health Assistant

Enter a symptom and get basic guidance on likely causes and next steps.

🏛️

Government Schemes

Browse livestock and dairy schemes farmers may be eligible for.

📊

Dashboard

A single view of total farmers, animals, and pending vaccinations.
